## Security Laws

| Name                                                       | Type                    | Applies To                           | Purpose / Focus                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **PCI-DSS**                                                | Security Standard       | Organizations handling card payments | Protect cardholder data (credit/debit/ATM/POS)  |
| **ISO 27001**                                              | International Standard  | All organizations                    | Establish & maintain ISMS using risk management |
| **ISO 27002**                                              | Best Practice Standard  | Organizations implementing ISMS      | Security controls & objectives guidance         |
| **HIPAA**                                                  | US Law                  | Healthcare sector                    | Protect patient health information              |
| **Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX)**                               | US Law                  | Public companies                     | Financial transparency & fraud prevention       |
| **Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)**                | US Law                  | Digital content & platforms          | Prevent copyright circumvention & piracy        |
| **Federal Information Security Modernization Act (FISMA)** | US Law                  | US Government agencies               | Federal information security governance         |
| **NIST SP 800-53**                                         | Control Catalog         | Federal & private systems            | Security & privacy control implementation       |
| **Federal IT Acquisition Reform Act (FITARA)**             | US Law                  | US Government IT                     | Reform & optimize IT procurement                |
| **COBIT**                                                  | IT Governance Framework | Enterprises                          | Align IT with business & manage risk            |
| **Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)**                          | US Law                  | Financial institutions               | Protect customer financial data                 |
| **CSIRT**                                                  | Operational Team        | Any organization                     | Incident detection, response & recovery         |
| **ITIL**                                                   | ITSM Framework          | IT service providers                 | Standardize IT service management               |
